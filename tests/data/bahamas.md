# The Bahamas

The Bahamas is a country in the West Indies within the Lucayan Archipelago. It consists of more than 700 islands, cays, and islets in the Atlantic Ocean, north of Cuba and southeast of Florida.

**Geography**

The largest islands include Andros, Grand Bahama, and New Providence, where the capital Nassau is located. The country is known for clear waters, coral reefs, and sandy beaches.

**Economy and tourism**

Tourism and offshore finance drive the economy. The Bahamas is a popular destination for cruise ships, diving, and beach holidays. The official language is English.
