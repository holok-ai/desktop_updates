# Fiji

Fiji is an island country in Melanesia, part of Oceania in the South Pacific Ocean. It lies about 1,100 nautical miles northeast of New Zealand. The archipelago consists of more than 330 islands and 500 islets.

**Geography and climate**

The two major islands are Viti Levu and Vanua Levu. The capital, Suva, is on Viti Levu. Fiji has a tropical marine climate with warm temperatures year-round and a distinct wet season from November to April.

**Economy and culture**

Fiji’s economy relies on tourism, sugar exports, and fisheries. Fijian culture blends indigenous traditions with Indian, European, and other Pacific influences. English, Fijian, and Fiji Hindi are widely spoken.
