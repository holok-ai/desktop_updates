# Saba

Saba is a Caribbean island and special municipality of the Netherlands. It lies in the northern Leeward Islands and is the smallest populated island of the former Netherlands Antilles.

**Geography**

The island has a land area of about 13 square kilometres and is dominated by Mount Scenery, a dormant volcano and the highest point in the Kingdom of the Netherlands. The main settlement is The Bottom.

**Culture and access**

Saba is known for diving, hiking, and a quiet, small-scale way of life. There is no significant beach; the island is reached by boat or small aircraft. English and Dutch are both used.
